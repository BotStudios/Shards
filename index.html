Team

Joe, formerly Chee Yong Lee started this organization,

team since 2019 and the aim was to be creative, and collaborative.

With just a few members, we started our first ever project, a Discord Bot.

Its was named Jet Brawley and it started as a multi-purpose Discord Bot,

soon we start to making more Discord bots (shortem, modmailbot, .etc)

and they're open source, this has become one of our aim not long ago.

With all that said, now we are not just a team of Discord Bot developers,

as we no longer focus on making Discord bots only, and our second

organization, The Coding Studios, aims to make all kinds of projects.
